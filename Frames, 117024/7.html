Electronics & Communication Engineering Departemnt

About the Department:

The Department of ECE was established in 2004 and permanently recognized by JNTU Hyderabad. The department has created a student association called “Futuronics” under the aegis of which the students have successfully conducted the events like Technical Quizes, Mini Project exhibition, Guest lectures, Tech fest, Technical Symposium to quote a few. The department has been accredited by NBA with Tier-I, AICTE, New Delhi for both UG &PG (M.Tech-VLSI&ES) Programmes. The strengths of the department are the specialized laboratories, senior professors, dynamic faculty and meritorious students. The Department has been organizing the Faculty Development Programmes in every semester in Collaboration with IIT-KGP,IIT-Bombay and NIT-Patna. The department is being recognized as “Research centre” (PhD-ECE) by JNTUH in 2017-18. The Department has well equipped Laboratories, state of the art laboratory facilities in the fields of Analog circuits, Communications, Image processing, VLSI. The department also facilitated with Centre of Excellence in IOT, Robotics. The department has an IEEE student branch, IETE student forum to facilitate effective interaction with the industry and academia through seminars, workshops. The department has created a student association called “Electrosurge” under the aegis of which the students have successfully conducted the events like Technical Quizes, Mini Project exhibition, Guest lectures, Tech fest, Technical Symposium to quote a few.
