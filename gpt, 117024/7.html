Electronics & Communication Engineering Department

About the Department:

The Department of ECE was established in 2004 and permanently recognized by JNTU Hyderabad.

The department has created a student association called “Futuronics” under the aegis of which the students have successfully conducted events like Technical Quizzes, Mini Project exhibitions, Guest lectures, Tech fests, Technical Symposiums, to name a few.

The department has been accredited by NBA with Tier-I, AICTE, New Delhi for both UG & PG (M.Tech-VLSI&ES) Programmes.

The strengths of the department include specialized laboratories, senior professors, dynamic faculty, and meritorious students.

The Department has been organizing Faculty Development Programmes every semester in Collaboration with IIT-KGP, IIT-Bombay, and NIT-Patna.

The department is recognized as a “Research centre” (PhD-ECE) by JNTUH since 2017-18.

The Department has well-equipped Laboratories, state-of-the-art laboratory facilities in the fields of Analog circuits, Communications, Image processing, VLSI. The department also facilitates a Centre of Excellence in IOT and Robotics.

The department has an IEEE student branch, IETE student forum to facilitate effective interaction with industry and academia through seminars and workshops.

The department has created a student association called “Electrosurge” under the aegis of which the students have successfully conducted events like Technical Quizzes, Mini Project exhibitions, Guest lectures, Tech fests, Technical Symposiums, to name a few.
